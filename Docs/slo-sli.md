# SLO and SLI

## Service

Order Service is a demo API used to practice real-time SRE monitoring, alerting, logging, and incident response.

## What Are SLIs?

SLI means Service Level Indicator.

An SLI is a measurement of service reliability.

Examples:

availability
latency
error rate
throughput

## What Are SLOs?

SLO means Service Level Objective.

An SLO is the target reliability level we want to maintain.

Example: 99.9% availability


## SLOs Objective & Target

Availability:-	 99.9% monthly uptime
Latency:-	     95% of requests complete under 300ms
Error Rate:-	 Less than 1% of requests return 5xx
Recovery:-	     MTTR under 15 minutes
